Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna recipe has been a hit in my family. The cheeses melt into an incredibly delicious flavorful bite. This is a recipe that the whole family will be coming back to.

Meat Sauce Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 28oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounce tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatos
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large green pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Make Lasagna

  • 1/2 pound lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 grated Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Boil Water for Pasta

    2. Bring water to a boil add salt then add the Pasta

  2. Cook the Ground Beef

    3. As the noodles are boiling begin cooking the ground beef. Add the 2 tablespoons of olive oil and the ground beef in a large pan. Cook until browned.

  3. Add the pepper, onions, garlic, and the beef

    4. Add the diced pepper, onions and garlic, saute until browned. Add the beef into the pan and turn heat on simmer for 5 minutes.

  4. Transfer to pot add tomatos and other ingredients

    5. Add the crushed tomatos, beef and other ingredients to the pot Stir in a small amount of sugar and put heat to medium. Add salt to taste and bring the pot to a simmer. Cook for 15 to 45 minutes. Remove from heat

  5. Boil and drain the noodles

    6. Bring the noodles to a boil and drain when tender

  6. Heat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Create Lasagna

    8. Layer the noodles on the bottom of the baking pan. Add a layer of sauce. Add more noodles. Add more sauce. Repeat this layering process until ending at the top layer with cheese.

  8. Bake lasagna
  9. Cool and Serve
