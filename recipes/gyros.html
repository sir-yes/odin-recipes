Gyro Recipe

Gyros

Description

Gyros are a greek sandwhich that are enjoyed by many. This dish is a combination of and beef and lamb baked in a water bath to ensure the meat stays moist.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pound of ground lamb
  • 1 medium onion, chopped rouhgly
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp dried marjoram
  • 1 Tbsp dried ground rosemary
  • 2 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop onion or use a food processor. Squeeze the juice out in a kitchen towel.
  2. Add meat, onion, garlic, spices, salt and pepper to a large bowl and mix thorougly.
  3. Bake in a water bath (place load pan inside another pan filled with water) at 325 degrees Fahrenheiht for about an hour or until internal temperature reaches 165F-170F.
  4. Remove from oven, drain fat and place on a cooling rack
  5. Cover meat with aluminum foil and a heavy weight for about 15 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 175F.
  6. Place meat on a cutting board and slice as thinly as possible.
  7. heat the thin slices on a frying pan to brown.
  8. Warm pita in another pan.
  9. Place meat on the pita and taziki sauce.
  10. Enjoy with rice pilaf or a greek salad.
