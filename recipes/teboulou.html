Teboulou

Description

A fresh salad that is a great addition to the hot summer picnic or at home. With a parsley base this salad has a strong parsley flavor with a hint of lemon. This will become a staple in your families summertime salads.

Ingredients

  • 1 bundle of curly parsley
  • 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • A few radishes
  • 2 roma tomatos
  • 1 English cucumber

Steps

  1. Trim Parsley

    2. Remove parsley leaves from the stems. Discard the stems.

  2. Cut vegetables

    3. Finely dice the english cucmber, radish, roma tomato

  3. Mix and Enjoy

    4. Mix the parsley, vegetables, and olive oil. Serve cold.
